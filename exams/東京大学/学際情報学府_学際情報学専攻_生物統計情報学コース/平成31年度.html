(1-8)

エ. 1/p + 1/(1-p)

なぜなら、 \begin{align} \frac{d}{dp} \log \frac{p}{1-p} &= \frac{d}{dp} \left( \log p - \log (1-p) \right) \\ &= \frac{1}{p} - \frac{-1}{1-p} \\ &= \frac{1}{p} + \frac{1}{1-p} \end{align}
